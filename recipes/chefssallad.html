Chef's Salad with Grilled Chicken

This is a classic ranch dressing ratio, so it hits your taste buds the way it should. The black pepper is not oppressive as it's balanced out by the tangy buttermilk and creamy mayo. Can't go wrong with this!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
  • 1/4 cup whole buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons chopped fresh chives
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons chopped fresh dill
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon grated or minced garlic
  • 5 ounces butter lettuce, torn
  • 3 cups chopped iceberg lettuce
  • 1 cup thinly sliced English cucumber
  • 4 ounces sharp Cheddar cheese, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 medium avocado - peeled, pitted and sliced
  • 2 large hard-boiled eggs, peeled and quartered
